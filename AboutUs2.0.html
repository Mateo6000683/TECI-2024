Welcome to our
Virtual Reality hub!

We are a group of students in multimedia engineering from the Universidad Militar Nueva Granada. Our focus specializes in the branches of design and programming. In this case, we wanted to bring this knowledge to the web design approach of a page whose central axis is virtual reality. This project is a way to make people understand what this technology is, its applications in the modern world, and the range it can have in the future with the constant advancement of science.
