"The World simulation in a different environment"

Virtual Reality is an experience that gathers different elements to create a virtual environment simulating a new world, with the aim to give new forms of presenting products to users.

3D Models

To achieve everything the VR propose, this technology implements models and environments 3D, being an approach to what a real environment could be

IMMERSION

The most remarkable thing about immersion is the feeling that about finding a whole new world for the user, these kinds of feelings are a priority for the new emerging environments.

INTERACTIVITY

The objects inside VR are made most of the time for the user to get a better approach to the product and to reinforce the feeling of immersion with emerging text, object movement, etc.
